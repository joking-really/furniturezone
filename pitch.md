## 2. Pain Points Identified

### 2.1 High-Ticket Purchase Anxiety
- Furniture is a high-consideration purchase (Rs. 5,000 - Rs. 155,000+)
- Customers need reassurance on quality, material, dimensions, delivery
- No instant answers = abandoned carts

### 2.2 WhatsApp Inquiry Overload
- They use WhatsApp click-to-chat (visible in site code)
- Phone number +92-306-4757194 gets constant inquiries
- Manual replies on dimensions, availability, delivery timelines
- No automated qualification or follow-up

### 2.3 Cart Abandonment
- WooCommerce cart but no recovery system
- 70%+ cart abandonment rate typical for furniture e-commerce
- No automated "complete your order" or "price drop" alerts

### 2.4 Limited Sales Hours
- Showroom open Mon-Sat 10 AM - 9 PM
- Online inquiries come 24/7 — no after-hours support
- International/expat customers in different time zones

### 2.5 No Lead Nurturing
- Customer browses 3 beds, adds to cart, leaves
- No follow-up: "Still deciding? Here's a similar option on sale"
- No WhatsApp broadcast for new arrivals or promotions

### 2.6 Manual Order Updates
- Customers call/WhatsApp for order status
- "Where is my delivery?" — repetitive, time-consuming
- No automated shipping/delivery notifications

## 3. What Axovion Can Build

### 3.1 AI WhatsApp Sales Bot (Primary Hook)
**The Problem:** Every WhatsApp message is a potential Rs. 50,000+ sale that needs instant, knowledgeable response.
**Our Solution:**
- AI chatbot on Furniturezone's WhatsApp Business number
- Answers product questions: dimensions, material, colors, availability
- Recommends products based on room size, budget, style preference
- Qualifies buyers: budget range, timeline, delivery location
- Sends product catalogs and photos automatically
- Escalates to human for custom orders
- Urdu + English bilingual

**ROI:** 60% faster response → 35% more conversions → 20% higher average order value

### 3.2 Abandoned Cart Recovery
**Problem:** 7 out of 10 carts are abandoned.
**Solution:**
- Track cart abandonment in real-time
- WhatsApp message after 1 hour: "You left something beautiful behind..."
- Include product image + 5% discount code
- Follow-up after 24 hours with similar recommendations

### 3.3 Order Tracking Automation
**Problem:** "Where is my order?" eats support time.
**Solution:**
- Customer messages "order status" → bot pulls from WooCommerce
- Automated delivery updates via WhatsApp
- "Your Cambridge King Bed will arrive tomorrow between 2-4 PM"

### 3.4 AI Voice Agent for Phone Inquiries
**Problem:** +92-306-4757194 rings after hours.
**Solution:**
- AI voice agent answers calls 24/7
- Natural Urdu/English conversation
- Answers FAQs, books showroom visits, takes messages

### 3.5 Smart CRM for Furniture Sales
**Problem:** No customer history or follow-up system.
**Solution:**
- Custom CRM dashboard
- Customer profiles: viewed products, past orders, preferences
- Automated re-engagement: "It's been 6 months — time for a dining upgrade?"
- Birthday/anniversary discounts
